# Meeting Task Tool - Simple Overview

## What It Does

Turn meeting notes into organized, trackable tasks automatically.

---

## How It Works

### Step 1: Upload Your Meeting
Paste your meeting transcript or notes into the app.

### Step 2: AI Extracts Tasks
The app automatically finds:
- What needs to be done1
- Who should do it
- When it's due
- How important it is

### Step 3: Review & Manage
See all your tasks in one place. Edit anything that needs fixing.

### Step 4: Track Progress
View tasks in a table or timeline chart. Mark them as done when complete.
